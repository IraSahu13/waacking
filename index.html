What is Waacking

Waacking is a form of street dance created in the LGBT clubs of Los Angeles during the 1970s disco era.It is influenced by many forms of movements and dance such as jazz, gymnastics and martial arts.It is all about rotational hand movements, posing and expresiveness. Just feel you body and express yourself!
